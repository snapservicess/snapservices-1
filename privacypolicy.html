Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 12, 2024

SnapService ("we," "us," or "our") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use our website:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, city, zip code, and other identifiable details when you create an account or book a service.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our site, such as IP addresses, browser type, device information, and pages visited.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience, analyze usage patterns, and personalize content.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To process your service requests and bookings.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.
  • To personalize your user experience and tailor content to your preferences.
  • To send important notices, such as updates, alerts, and changes to our policies.
  • For analytics and improving the functionality of our services.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your personal information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and delivering services to you.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a legal process.
  • Business Transfers: If SnapService undergoes a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, and destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the personal information we have about you.
  • Correction: You can update or correct your personal information if it is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain legal restrictions.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of receiving marketing communications from us at any time by using the unsubscribe option or contacting us directly.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. These third-party websites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for the content or practices of these websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before submitting any personal information.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make changes, we will post the updated Privacy Policy on this page with a new effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
